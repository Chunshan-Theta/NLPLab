  
地點很好，就在台北車站新光三越的對面，麥當勞的樓上10樓。房間乾淨，早餐要去後面的2號店食，是breakfast set 而不是buffet, 不過味道ok。旅館旁邊有不少吃的商店，到台北車站坐捷運很方便。 
Very close to the Taipei station, within 10 mins walk. Room is clean and tidy. Price includes breakfast, served in set and not buffet style, tasted ok but I rather buy the local breakfast( Taiwanese style rice ball & egg pancake)  from the same street.
